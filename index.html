about.

“PreCon Global is a comprehensive pre-construction planning and solutions provider for the construction industry worldwide.” We offer a wide array of pre-construction services ranging from quantity takeoffs, cost estimation, project scheduling, and statistical data analysis for Commercial, Residential, and Infrastructure projects. Our team carries over 20 years of experience in delivering pre-construction services to our clients in India, and the United States. We are capable of delivering quantity takeoffs, budgets, and schedules to our clients through our dedicated and talented team of engineers and architects who work closely with each client to deliver their pre-construction tasks. Our services are aimed at obtaining maximum information from available resources and deliver a realistic and constructible solution to the Owners, Architects, Engineers, and Contractors. We believe in keeping up with the fast-paced technological advancements occurring in the construction industry and use the most suitable technology to deliver useful information to our clients.

services.

We provide a host of pre-construction services to the Architectural Engineering and Construction industries.

Material Quantity Take-off

We work on the latest available software to provide with the most accurate quantity takeoffs. Our delivery of results to clients are unmatched in the industry with a maximum turn-around time of 24-48 hours only! We achieve this by assigning dedicated professionals to work independently with each client while the work progress is simultaneously monitored through a series of quality checks to ensure accuracy in our work. If you are looking for a quick and accurate delivery of quantity takeoffs, give us an opportunity to demonstrate our capabilities by sending us an email.

Cost Estimation

Our team of highly talented estimators can deliver construction budgets and preliminary estimates as per the governing codes, prevailing tax rates, and based on a thorough understanding of the competitive markets surrounding you. We specialize in concrete precast production and logistic costs, commercial tenant improvements, deep energy retrofit, and high-rise residential structures.

Project Scheduling

Need a quick preliminary construction schedule to present to the client? Or do you need a schedule to go along with your cost estimate & RFP packag? We can do it for you. And we can do it quick! Our construction schedulers can work with the available information (at any stage of a project's lifecycle) to determine the best construction sequencing and scheduling by coordinating with your team.

Statistical Data Analysis

Do you have a huge chunk of historical data sitting idle in your computers and you do not know what to do with it? Give us an opportunity to work with your data and yield useful information using statistical analysis. We can work on your historical cost estimates, actual cost of construction activities, and many other data to provide useful insights that could help you make informed decisions in the future.As it is rightly said, "A company is only as good as its data." - Anonymous, let us dig deep into your data and reveal interesting facts for the betterment of your organization!
